One vision. Infinite possibilities.

If we really want to solve the greatest challenges facing our world, we have to invest in people. We have to invest in leaders and innovators. We have to invest in engineers.

And as an alumnus, he knows that the University of Florida is the best place to start. It’s where he started. It’s where you started.

When we invest in one vision together, the possibilities are infinite.
“In the future nearly all our problems will be solved by engineering...
We are going to create the future together.”
– Dr. Herbert “Herbie” Wertheim
