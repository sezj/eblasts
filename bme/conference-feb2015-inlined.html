Conference: rtFIN 2015
Real-time Functional Imaging and Neurofeedback
February 12-13, 2015
University of Florida Hilton Conference Center
Gainesville, Florida, USA

On behalf of Dr. Christine Schmidt, the Chair of the Biomedical Engineering, University of Florida, and Dr. Cammy Abernathy, the Dean of the College of Engineering, University of Florida, and the organizing committee of this conference, I welcome you to the International Conference on Real-time Functional Imaging and Neurofeedback (rtFIN).

The conference aims to bring together neuroscientists, engineers, computer scientists and clinicians interested in research and application of the Brain Machine Interfaces (BMIs) and Neurofeedback. To broaden perspectives and facilitate cross-pollination, the content of this version of the conference will include BMIs and Neurofeedback using all modalities of neuroimaging, including EEG/MEG, fNIRS and ECoG.
